# Humanizer (Basic)

Rewrite text so it reads like a real person wrote it — plain, natural, and free of the patterns that scream "AI." This is the **minimalist** version: clear rules, light touch, no over-correction.

## When to use
- "Humanize this" / "make it sound less like AI" / "this reads like ChatGPT"
- Polishing AI-drafted copy, emails, posts, or articles before publishing

**Not for:** changing the *meaning*, faking a specific person's voice, or evading AI-detection for dishonest purposes (don't claim a human wrote something they didn't).

## The tells to kill
- **Em-dash overuse** — and the —stacked— interruptions. Use commas, periods, or parentheses.
- **Cliché vocabulary:** delve, tapestry, realm, landscape, navigate, foster, leverage, robust, seamless, elevate, unlock, embark, testament, beacon, vibrant.
- **Formula phrases:** "In today's fast-paced world", "It's important to note that", "When it comes to", "At the end of the day", "more than just".
- **The "not just X, but Y" / "it's not about X — it's about Y"** construction. Say the thing directly.
- **Rule-of-three everything** ("fast, simple, and reliable"). Vary it; sometimes one word is enough.
- **Hedging pile-ups:** "may potentially help to possibly". Commit or cut.
- **Emoji bullet headers** and overly tidy parallel lists. Let structure breathe.

## How to humanize
1. **Vary sentence length.** Mix short punches with longer lines. Uniform rhythm reads robotic.
2. **Use plain words.** "use" not "utilize", "help" not "facilitate", "about" not "regarding".
3. **Allow contractions** ("you're", "it's", "don't") in anything but the most formal text.
4. **Be concrete.** Swap abstract nouns for specific things and examples.
5. **Cut throat-clearing.** Delete intros like "It's worth noting that"; start with the point.
6. **Keep one voice.** Pick a register and hold it; don't drift formal→casual.
7. **Trim.** If a word isn't doing work, remove it.

## Before / after
**Before:** In today's fast-paced world, it's important to note that leveraging this robust, seamless solution can help you unlock your full potential — not just saving time, but transforming the way you work.
**After:** This saves you time and changes how you work.

**Before:** Our platform fosters a vibrant tapestry of tools to elevate your workflow.
**After:** Our tools make your workflow faster.

## Don't overcorrect
Natural ≠ sloppy. Don't add fake typos, forced slang, or filler to "seem human." Keep it clean and let the plain, varied prose do the work.
