# Products, collections & the catalog

## Products vs variants
- A **product** = one item's core details (title, description, media, price, SKU, inventory).
- **Variants** = options of the *same* product (size, color, material) — never create duplicate products for these; add options and Shopify generates the variant matrix, each with its own price/SKU/inventory/image.
- **Status:** **Active** (published), **Draft** (hidden/not ready), **Unlisted** (reachable only by direct URL). Set to Active when ready.
- Organize with **product type, vendor, tags** — these also power smart collections.

## Good product content
- Title: clear, specific. Description: benefits + specs, not fluff; scannable.
- **Media:** multiple high-quality images (consistent framing/background), alt text, video where useful.
- **Pricing:** price, compare-at price (for sales), cost per item (for margins).
- **Inventory:** track quantity, set SKU/barcode, choose whether to continue selling when out of stock.

## Collections
- **Manual** — you hand-pick each product (good for curated/seasonal sets).
- **Smart / automated** — auto-populated by conditions (tag, price, type, vendor, inventory). Set them once; new matching products join automatically.
- Collections power navigation, storefront sections, and merchandising. _Requires the Basic plan or above._

## Payments, shipping & taxes (essentials)
- **Payments:** Shopify Payments (native; country-dependent; needs identity + bank + 2FA) or a third-party gateway. Test a real transaction, then leave **test mode off**.
- **Shipping:** set locations, then rates by zone (flat, weight-based, or carrier-calculated) via shipping profiles.
- **Taxes:** configure the regions you're liable in; Shopify Tax automates rates where available. **Tax compliance is the merchant's responsibility** — this is not tax advice.

## Policies & pages
Add refund/return, privacy, terms of service, and shipping policies (Shopify templates are a starting point, not legal advice — have them reviewed), plus a contact page. Link them in the footer menu.
